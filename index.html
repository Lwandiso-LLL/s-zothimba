Founder & Director

Siyanda Zothimba

Click image to view full size

Siyanda Zothimba is the founding Director of Zothimba Attorneys Inc. Under his leadership, the firm delivers strategic, client-focused, and results-driven legal solutions across multiple areas of law.

He is an Admitted Attorney of the High Court (registered with the Legal Practice Council) and holds a Bachelor of Commerce in Law (BCom Law) and a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) from the University of Fort Hare.

Siyanda has extensive experience in criminal law, labour law, road accident fund (RAF) claims, family and divorce matters, medical negligence, corporate law, wills and estates, property law, and customary law. He has previously served as a candidate attorney at Legal Aid South Africa, a pre-assessment officer at the Road Accident Fund, and has undertaken various internships in public and private legal institutions.

He is committed to ensuring access to justice, delivering effective legal solutions, and mentoring his team to uphold the highest standards of ethics, integrity, and governance.
